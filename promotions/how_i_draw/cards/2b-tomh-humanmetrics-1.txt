
The images I draw are visual expressions Carl Jung's archetypes, as described in his treatise General Description of the Types.
He uses abstract terms, such as extraversion and introversion, and feeling and thinking, for essential mental functions making them well-suited to portrayal in abstract art.

It amazes me that something so simple could be so powerful!
